A final-stage PhD researcher and Applied AI researcher at [Trinity College Dublin](https://www.tcd.ie/), focused on extracting real-world value from state-of-the-art AI technologies — Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, and LLMs — by integrating them with established theories like Multi-Agent Systems.

With previous experience as an ML Intern at Intel and a Senior Software Developer at Commvault, I bring both strong research foundations and industry engineering expertise to my work.

- **Agentic AI** — LLMs, Computer Vision, autonomous systems
