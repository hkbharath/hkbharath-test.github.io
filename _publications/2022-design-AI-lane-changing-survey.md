This paper surveys AI-based lane changing models for Connected and Autonomous Vehicles (CAVs), covering classical control methods, rule-based approaches, and modern deep learning and reinforcement learning techniques. It analyses the state of the art and identifies open challenges for safe and efficient lane changing in CAV settings.
